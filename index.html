At Dover Summit Web Design, our goal is to offer simple, inexpensive solutions to meet your small business, organization, or personal website needs. Whether you're looking for a completely new website or updating an existing site, we want to make the process easy for you.

Supportability

Our goal is to provide you a website that's easy enough to be updated by anybody with even a basic level of tech-savvy. If you'd prefer not to get hands-on, we do provide services. We don't want to design the site and leave you hanging. We want to work with you throughout the life of the website to help you get results.

Hosting

We don't offer web hosting services ourselves, but we can work with you to help find a host that suits your needs. We don't take a commission from any host, and we're not going to try and hard sell you into any features you don't need.

E-commerce

The web hosts we work with have online selling capability built-in. We'll not only design the look of the store and load your products into the database, but we'll also handle the setup of the ordering, shipping, and marketing components.
